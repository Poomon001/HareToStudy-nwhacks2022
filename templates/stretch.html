Why should you stretch?

With many students are stuck at home in front of their computers, stretching is more important now than ever. Sitting for long periods of time can cause muscle pain in the neck, back, or shoulders; this not only impacts your physical health but also your mental health as it distracts you from your work. Stretching has the benefit of loosening your muscles, which is important because it makes you feel good, thereby increasing your productivity and mood. Furthermore, stretching increases serotonin levels which enhances ones ability to deal with stress.
